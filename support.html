Unpause - Intermittent Fasting Timer

How to Use Unpause

Choose a fasting protocol — Select from popular intermittent fasting schedules like 16:8, 18:6, 20:4, or 23:1.

Start your fast — Tap to begin your fasting timer. The app tracks your progress and shows milestones along the way.

Get notified — Receive a notification when your fasting window is complete.

Track your journey — View your fasting history and streaks to stay motivated.

Tips

  • Start with a gentler protocol (like 14:10) if you're new to fasting
  • Enable notifications to know exactly when your fast ends
  • Use the widget for quick access to your current fast status
