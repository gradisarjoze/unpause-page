Privacy Policy

Unpause - Intermittent Fasting Timer
Last updated: January 11, 2025

Overview

Unpause is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. This includes no personal information, no analytics, no tracking, no health data transmission, no device identifiers, and no location data.

Data Storage

All app data (fasting sessions, preferences) is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Notifications

The app may request permission to send local notifications when your fasting period ends. These are generated entirely on your device and do not involve external servers.

Third-Party Services

Unpause does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data because we do not collect any data.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
