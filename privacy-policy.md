# Privacy Policy

**Unpause - Intermittent Fasting Timer**

*Last updated: January 11, 2025*

## Overview

Unpause is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.** Specifically:

- No personal information (name, email, phone number)
- No account creation or authentication
- No analytics or tracking
- No health data transmitted to external servers
- No device identifiers or advertising IDs
- No location data
- No usage analytics

## Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device only:

- Fasting session history
- Your selected fasting protocol
- App preferences (theme settings)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

## Notifications

The app may request permission to send local notifications to alert you when your fasting period ends. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers.

## Third-Party Services

Unpause does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

## Data Sharing

We do not share any data because we do not collect any data.

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
